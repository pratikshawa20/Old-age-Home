Food and Nutrition




In old-age homes, food is served in a mess-style cafeteria. You can choose between veg or non-veg food depending on your dietary preferences. However, there are no facilities for you to cook on your own.

Old-age Home is highly-skilled cooks, exercising their culinary craft in a clean and hygienic environment, ensure your food is always novel, appetising and above all, healthy. There are dedicated, segregated kitchens preparing vegetarian and non-vegetarian food, and scrupulous care is taken to ensure all linen and cutlery, both in the kitchens and at the dining hall, is also segregated and spotlessly clean.

Join your friends for breakfast, lunch or dinner at the dining hall, with a different menu every day to tickle your taste and fancy. And should you desire the flavour of home-cooked food, we provide a kitchenette in the dining hall for private use of residents, so you can prepare your favourite dish in your own way, whenever you want.
